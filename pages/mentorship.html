FAQ

Questions About
Mentorship

Do I need any experience?

Zero experience required. Our mentorship is designed specifically for beginners. If you have a passion and 5–10 hours per week, we can build something real together.

What platforms do you work with?

YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Twitch primarily. We recommend starting with 1–2 platforms and expanding once you have a system in place.

How long until I see results?

Most clients see meaningful growth within 60–90 days. Your first income streams typically activate in months 4–5. Full-time income timelines vary, but our average client hits that goal within 12–18 months.

What does it cost?

Mentorship starts at $497/month for the group program and $997/month for 1-on-1. Book a free call and we'll recommend the right fit for you.

Can I do this while working a full-time job?

Absolutely. Most of our clients start part-time. We'll build a workflow that fits your schedule — typically 5–10 hours per week is enough to grow meaningfully.
